How does this currency converter work?

MONIMEZ currency converter will show you how much your money is worth in other currencies at the real exchange rate.

You can convert over 5 currencies quickly and easily.

Simply input your amount in the currency at the calculator located below the main page and our tool will convert your currency at the mid-market rate (that’s the one you’ll find on Google).

Send money abroad using Wise and we’ll use the same rate you see on our currency converter.
